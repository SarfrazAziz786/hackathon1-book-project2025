# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This book provides a complete educational journey from foundational concepts to advanced implementations in humanoid robotics.

## About This Book

This resource is designed for engineers, researchers, and enthusiasts looking to understand and implement humanoid robots using modern AI techniques. We'll explore the integration of artificial intelligence with physical robotic systems to create truly autonomous machines.

## Book Structure

The book is organized into four comprehensive modules:

### Module 1: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)
Understanding ROS 2 as the middleware layer connecting AI agents to humanoid robot hardware and simulators. Learn about messaging, services, and actions that form the backbone of robotic systems.

### Module 2: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)
Building digital twins for humanoid robots using simulation. Master physics simulation, sensor modeling, and virtual environments for testing humanoid robotics algorithms.

Integrating vision, language, and action for humanoid robot autonomy. Master the latest techniques in multimodal AI for robot control.

Each module builds upon the previous ones to provide a comprehensive understanding of creating intelligent humanoid robots. Let's begin this journey into the future of robotics!
